On **World Water Day — March 22** — Sanjeevani Parivar launched the special **'Plastic-Free Vasai'** campaign to combat single-use plastic pollution.

## Campaign Activities

- **Awareness Rally** — Marches across various parts of Vasai city
- **Street Plays** — Dramatizations highlighting the harmful effects of plastic
- **Poster Exhibitions** — Displayed in schools and public places
- **Plastic Collection Drive** — Old plastic items collected for responsible disposal

## Public Response

Over **1,000 citizens** took the pledge to stop using single-use plastic. School students acted as brand ambassadors for the campaign, spreading awareness among their families and neighborhoods.

## Why It Matters

Vasai's coastline and local water bodies have been severely impacted by plastic pollution. This campaign aims to:

1. Raise awareness about plastic's environmental damage
2. Promote use of cloth bags and reusable containers
3. Build a community of responsible citizens

Sanjeevani Parivar is committed to continuing this campaign **throughout the year**. Join us in making Vasai plastic-free!

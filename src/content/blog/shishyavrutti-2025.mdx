The **Education Division** of Sanjeevani Parivar annually awards scholarships to deserving students from economically weaker sections of society.

## This Year's Selection

This year, **50 students** were selected based on the following criteria:

- Academic performance (percentage of marks)
- Financial background
- Teacher recommendations

## Scholarship Structure

| Grade | Annual Scholarship |
|-------|--------------------|
| 5th to 7th | ₹2,000/year |
| 8th to 10th | ₹3,500/year |
| College level | ₹5,000/year |

## Ceremony Highlights

The distribution ceremony was a proud moment for students and their families. Guest speakers encouraged the students to work hard and give back to society.

> "Education is the most powerful weapon you can use to change the world."

Keeping the lamp of education burning is our mission and our joy.

Sanjeevani Parivar conducts an **annual tree plantation drive** every monsoon season. This year, over **500 saplings** were planted across Vasai and surrounding areas.

## Who Participated

- Students from local schools
- Parents and teachers
- Community volunteers

## Species Planted

A variety of native tree species were chosen, including Neem, Peepal, Mango, and Jackfruit — all well-suited to the local climate and beneficial to the ecosystem.

## Follow-up Care

A special monitoring committee has been formed to ensure the saplings are watered and tended to regularly. Each volunteer "adopted" a tree and committed to its growth.

> "Planting a tree is an act of hope for the future." — Sanjeevani Parivar

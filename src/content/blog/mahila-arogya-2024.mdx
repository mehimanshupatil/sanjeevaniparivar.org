Sanjeevani Parivar organized a **one-day workshop** to create awareness about women's health. The event brought together expert doctors and health professionals to guide women on key health topics.

## Expert Speakers

- **Gynecologist** — Women's specific health issues and preventive care
- **Nutritionist** — Importance of balanced diet during all life stages
- **Psychiatrist** — Mental health and stress management

## Workshop Sessions

1. **Nutrition & Diet** — Understanding nutritional needs at different life stages
2. **Exercise & Fitness** — Simple routines that fit into daily life
3. **Mental Health** — Breaking stigma, recognizing stress, seeking help
4. **Preventive Checkups** — Why regular screening matters

## Participation

Over **200 women** attended the workshop. Participants appreciated the practical, accessible advice provided by the doctors in a comfortable and supportive environment.

> "Knowing our health is the first step to protecting it."

Women's empowerment is an integral part of Sanjeevani Parivar's work. Stay tuned for our next workshop!
